Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TORBU, LLC (“TORBU,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or contact us.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you submit a contact form or email us. This may include your name, organization, email address, and message content.

We may also collect limited technical information automatically (for example, browser type, device information, and basic usage data) to operate and improve the site.

How we use information

We use information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and communicate with you
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website
  • Prevent abuse, spam, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us run the site (for example, hosting, email delivery, and security providers) strictly for those purposes and subject to appropriate safeguards.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or to prevent fraud or abuse.

Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for essential site functionality and to understand site usage. If we use analytics, it is intended to be privacy-respecting and focused on aggregate trends.

You can usually manage cookies through your browser settings.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to maintain appropriate safeguards.

Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, and protect the integrity of our services.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have provided, subject to applicable law and legitimate business needs.

Children

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date indicates the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact TORBU at info@torbu.com.
